About Refresh Meds


Because our health is a fundamental part of being human. Without it, we have nothing.



The Union health ministry reveals that medicines are the biggest financial burden on Indian households. 38 million Indians fall below the poverty line due to spending on medicines alone. As in today's situation everyone, either literate, illiterate, poor or rich, everyone is concerned about his/her health . But the truth is that a huge percent of the population cannot pay or afford expensive medicines if at all they are necessary for them.Helping the poor and needy people with ease in access to medicine. Reducing wastage of medicine. Proper disposal of drugs before and after expiration. Our mission is to serve vulnerable populations through responsible collection and distribution of donated medicines.


That’s why we’re here. Refresh Med provides access so everyone gets the care they deserve.
