Why medicine?


Because our health is a fundamental part of being human. Without it, we have nothing.



The Union health ministry reveals that medicines are the biggest financial burden on Indian households. 38 million Indians fall below the poverty line due to spending on medicines alone. As in today's situation everyone, either literate, illiterate, poor or rich, everyone is concerned about his/her health . But the truth is that a huge percent of the population cannot pay or afford expensive medicines if at all they are necessary for them.
